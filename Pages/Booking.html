JUST A BOOKING

Step into the world of speculative design! As part of my exciting journey through the UX Design Institute, I embarked on a captivating case study focused on creating a cutting-edge hotel booking website. Join me as I unleash my creativity and UX expertise, craft an extraordinary online platform specifically tailored to meet the unique needs of travelers with special requirements. My mission? To seamlessly connect these remarkable individuals with their ideal accommodations, making their dream stays a reality. Get ready to embark on a transformative experience where innovation and inclusivity converge.

User Reasearch

Competitive Bench-marketing

Engage in a fascinating expedition with me as we delve into the world of competitors and decipher the expectations of our future users. Armed with a curious spirit, I embarked on a captivating journey through the virtual realm, exploring renowned hotels and perusing prominent booking websites like Booking.com and Hilton Hotels, among others.

Amidst this virtual exploration, a remarkable revelation unfolded: the power of advertisements in shaping the user experience. While an onslaught of advertisements can overwhelm and frustrate users, a strategic blend of non-intrusive ads emerged as a game-changer. Imagine the joy of stumbling upon alluring discounts for off-season bookings, seamlessly enhancing the user's travel experience. Ah, the sweet balance between practicality and delight!

As my adventure continued, I diligently recorded the mental models expected from our future users, juxtaposed with my personal encounters. These observations paved the way for a comprehensive list of pain points and, of course, the elation of positive experiences. Armed with this treasure trove of insights, I embarked on the next phase of my quest: the mighty affinity diagram.

SurveyMonkey

In my relentless pursuit of understanding user behaviors during the hotel booking process, I turned to the versatile tool known as SurveyMonkey. This invaluable resource enabled me to collect users' feedback, granting me profound insights into their expectations and desires when engaging in the booking experience. With each response, a piece of the puzzle fell into place, illuminating the path toward a user-centric design.

As the survey data flowed in, I meticulously integrated it into my trusted affinity diagram. This visual representation served as a canvas, where patterns and connections emerged, providing a holistic view of user preferences and pain points. The synergy between the survey data and the affinity diagram propelled me closer to crafting a truly exceptional user experience.

However, even in the face of progress, I acknowledge the inherent limitations of my sample size. Though the survey added valuable structure to my research, I yearn to broaden the scope in future endeavors. To achieve this, I aspire to leverage diverse platforms for survey distribution, expanding the reach and diversity of respondents.

If circumstances permit, I aim to allocate resources to engage a target audience specifically aligned with our user demographic, ensuring a more representative and comprehensive data set. Through these enhanced methodologies, I endeavor to deepen my understanding, enrich the insights gained, and forge stronger connections with the needs and desires of our users. By continually refining and expanding our research approach, we lay the foundation for a truly remarkable booking experience.

Usability Testing

The art of usability testing unveils a profound understanding of user desires and ensures that the features and overarching purpose of a hotel booking platform align seamlessly with their expectations. By observing real-life individuals as they engage in the process of booking a hotel stay, we gain invaluable insights into their behaviors, needs, and mental models, paving the way for a truly user-centric design.

In my unwavering pursuit of excellence, I meticulously conducted two prerecorded usability tests, keenly documenting every observation and revelation. These firsthand encounters provided a wealth of information, enriching my understanding of user interactions and shedding light on areas that require refinement. Moreover, driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I embarked on a personal usability test, immersing myself in the experience of booking a hotel stay. The culmination of my efforts resulted in a captivating 60-minute recording, a testament to my dedication and commitment to uncovering every nuance of the user journey.

Through the power of usability testing, we fortify our understanding of user expectations, ensuring that our design decisions are grounded in real-world insights. As we continuously fine-tune our platform, guided by these valuable experiences, we forge an unbreakable bond between user and design, creating a hotel booking experience that exceeds all expectations.

Findings

Challenges

    Challenges: During my research, I discovered that users encountered various challenges while using different hotel booking websites. Although each user had unique needs and experiences, there were common hurdles that many of them faced. These challenges included:
  • Too much scrolling: Users found it tiresome to scroll through lengthy pages to find the desired information or navigate to different sections of the website.
  • Difficulty accessing the map: The map feature, which is crucial for users to locate hotels in their preferred areas, was not easily accessible or prominently displayed on the websites.
  • Lack of hotel and room comparison: Users expressed frustration when attempting to compare hotels and rooms side by side, as it was not a seamless or intuitive process on the booking websites.
  • Confusing add-ons: Users encountered difficulties in understanding the various add-ons or additional services offered by hotels. The lack of clear explanations and options for more detailed information caused confusion and hindered decision-making.
  • Challenges in changing dates and location: Users faced hurdles when trying to modify their booking dates or select a different location, which caused frustration and hindered the overall user experience.

Solutions

    To address these challenges and enhance the user experience, I have devised the following solutions: -Condense information on a single page:
  • By optimizing the layout and design, I aim to minimize the need for excessive scrolling, allowing users to access key information more efficiently.
  • Prominently display the map: I propose placing the map feature in a prominent and easily accessible spot, ensuring that users can swiftly locate hotels in their desired areas.
  • Implement a hotel and room comparison feature: To facilitate the decision-making process, I suggest providing a clear and intuitive way for users to compare hotels and rooms, enabling them to make informed choices.
  • Provide clear explanations for add-ons: I recommend offering concise explanations of add-ons, accompanied by the option for users to access more detailed information if desired. This will alleviate confusion and help users make informed decisions.
  • Enable easy modification of dates and location: To enhance flexibility, I propose incorporating user-friendly options that allow users to modify their booking dates and select different locations effortlessly.

Analisis

Affinity Diagram

Ah, the power of the affinity diagram—a remarkable tool that brings order to the chaos of dense research data. By utilizing Mirro, I am able to seamlessly organize my collected data onto sticky notes and arrange them into logical groups. This process has taken me on a transformative journey of sense-making and comprehension.

The affinity diagram, brought to life through Mirro, acts as a guiding light, illuminating the way forward. It allows me to distill the vast amounts of information I've gathered into easily digestible bits. Each carefully arranged note weaves a tapestry of patterns, connections, and themes, providing a visual representation of the research findings. This organized depiction of my data paves the way for deeper understanding, allowing me to identify pain points with clarity and gain a clear direction for the website's design.

With the affinity diagram as my trusted compass, I navigate the intricate landscape of user experiences with confidence and purpose. By leveraging the insights gleaned from the affinity diagram, I am empowered to make informed decisions and prioritize areas of improvement. It serves as a roadmap for shaping the future of the website, ensuring a user-centered design that truly meets the needs and desires of my audience.

Customer journey map

With the data from my affinity diagram in hand, I embarked on the creation of a captivating customer journey map. This visual masterpiece serves as a dynamic storyline, illustrating the user's engagement with the website at every step. It brings their experiences to life, providing a holistic view of their interactions and emotions along the way.

The customer journey map acts as a powerful tool, enabling both myself and the client to visualize the areas that require improvement and optimize on the website's strong points. It paints a vivid picture of the user's entire experience, from initial touchpoints to the final goal, allowing us to identify pain points and moments of delight. Drawing upon the insights derived from the affinity diagram, I carefully crafted this customer journey map. Each step is thoughtfully plotted, capturing the essence of user interactions and highlighting key touchpoints that shape their overall perception. It serves as a roadmap for enhancing the user experience, guiding design decisions and prioritizing areas for improvement.

By presenting this visual representation to the client, I provide them with a clear understanding of the user's perspective. They can easily grasp the user's journey, visualize pain points, and identify opportunities for optimization. The customer journey map fosters collaboration and aligns stakeholders, setting the stage for meaningful discussions and informed decision-making

Design

Low fidelity

During this stage of the project, I delved into the realm of creativity and exploration. Armed with paper cutouts, drawings, and a playful mindset, I began to bring my design ideas to life. Through interactive prototypes, I tested and refined the concepts I envisioned for the website.

In this process of tactile experimentation, a realization struck me—I could simplify the booking process to make it even easier for users. With this newfound clarity, I swiftly made the necessary adjustments , streamlining the user journey and removing unnecessary complexities. The aim was to create a seamless and intuitive experience for anyone looking to book a hotel stay. Once I was satisfied with the redesigned flow, I captured the essence of the user journey in a dynamic gif This animated representation showcased the key interactions and transitions, offering a glimpse into the user experience. It served as both a visual demonstration and a means of sharing my design vision with stakeholders and team members.

The process of playing, testing, refining, and ultimately creating the gif was a joyful and iterative journey. It allowed me to iterate on my ideas and validate their effectiveness, ensuring that the final design would meet the needs and expectations of users.

Testing

Prototype

The mid-fidelity prototype I created serves as a primitive representation of the envisioned end product, allowing me to test and validate the usability and flow of the website. It strikes a balance between functionality and visual fidelity, providing a realistic user experience while still being relatively quick and cost-effective to produce.

Through rigorous testing and user feedback, I identified an opportunity to enhance the user's experience by incorporating a filter option. Recognizing the importance of helping users find the correct hotel and room, I made the necessary addition to the prototype. This filter functionality would enable users to refine their search based on specific criteria such as location, price range, amenities, and more. By integrating this filter option, I aimed to streamline the user's journey and make it easier for them to find their ideal accommodation. The filter would act as a powerful tool, empowering users to narrow down their choices and quickly locate the options that align with their preferences and needs.

Testing the prototype with the added filter option provided valuable insights into its effectiveness and usability. It allowed me to observe how users interacted with this new feature, gauging their satisfaction and identifying any potential pain points or areas for improvement.

Hand-over

Annotations

My annotations serve as a valuable resource, providing additional context and detailed explanations of the website design. By incorporating these annotations, I ensure that the developers have a crystal-clear vision of the desired end product's appearance and functionality.

But let's not forget the fun in the process! These annotations bring an element of excitement and playfulness to the table. They infuse the professional tone with a dash of creativity, making the reading experience enjoyable and engaging for both developers and stakeholders.

With my annotations in hand, developers gain valuable insights that guide them in bringing my design to life. Each annotation acts as a beacon, illuminating the intricacies of the design, the user interactions, and the overall experience I aim to achieve. By capturing these nuances, I empower the development team to create a product that perfectly aligns with my creative vision.


My Learings

During the creation of the flow chart, I initially included all the steps required to complete the booking process. However, as I progressed to develop the low-fidelity design, I realized that certain steps could be eliminated to simplify the process. By enhancing the descriptions of the hotels and rooms, users can make informed decisions without unnecessary steps.

In my effort to provide more information to users, I initially added an information button. However, through user testing, I discovered that this approach could lead to user frustration. To address this, I opted for a more streamlined approach. I incorporated a simple explanation directly accessible to users, while reserving more detailed information for the 'about' page. This ensures that users have the necessary information without overwhelming them with excessive details.

While working on the wireframe, I recognized the need to make adjustments to the flow. To improve user navigation and flexibility, I included additional options that allow users to easily move back and forth within the process. This enhances the overall usability of the website and provides a more intuitive experience.

Reflecting on my survey efforts, I realized that there were opportunities for improvement. The survey group was small, and the questions were not as well-thought-out as they could have been. To gather more meaningful feedback in the future, I will aim to expand the sample size and refine the survey questions, ensuring they are clear, comprehensive, and aligned with my research goals.

Thiss will result in more valuable insights that can inform the design process effectively. By recognizing and addressing these areas for improvement, I am committed to refining my design approach and delivering an exceptional user experience.
