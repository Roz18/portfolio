Engage in a fascinating expedition with me as we delve into the world of competitors and decipher the expectations of our future users. Findings

Challenges

Challenges: During my research, I discovered that users encountered various challenges while using different hotel booking websites. Although each user had unique needs and experiences, there were common hurdles that many of them faced. These challenges included: -Too much scrolling: Users found it tiresome to scroll through lengthy pages to find the desired information or navigate to different sections of the website. -Difficulty accessing the map: The map feature, which is crucial for users to locate hotels in their preferred areas, was not easily accessible or prominently displayed on the websites. -Lack of hotel and room comparison: Users expressed frustration when attempting to compare hotels and rooms side by side, as it was not a seamless or intuitive process on the booking websites. -Confusing add-ons: Users encountered difficulties in understanding the various add-ons or additional services offered by hotels. The lack of clear explanations and options for more detailed information caused confusion and hindered decision-making. -Challenges in changing dates and location: Users faced hurdles when trying to modify their booking dates or select a different location, which caused frustration and hindered the overall user experience.

Solutions

To address these challenges and enhance the user experience, I have devised the following solutions: -Condense information on a single page: By optimizing the layout and design, I aim to minimize the need for excessive scrolling, allowing users to access key information more efficiently. -Prominently display the map: I propose placing the map feature in a prominent and easily accessible spot, ensuring that users can swiftly locate hotels in their desired areas. -Implement a hotel and room comparison feature: To facilitate the decision-making process, I suggest providing a clear and intuitive way for users to compare hotels and rooms, enabling them to make informed choices. -Provide clear explanations for add-ons: I recommend offering concise explanations of add-ons, accompanied by the option for users to access more detailed information if desired. This will alleviate confusion and help users make informed decisions. -Enable easy modification of dates and location: To enhance flexibility, I propose incorporating user-friendly options that allow users to modify their booking dates and select different locations effortlessly.

Testing

Prototype

The mid-fidelity prototype I created serves as a primitive representation of the envisioned end product, allowing me to test and validate the usability and flow of the website. It strikes a balance between functionality and visual fidelity, providing a realistic user experience while still being relatively quick and cost-effective to produce. Through rigorous testing and user feedback, I identified an opportunity to enhance the user's experience by incorporating a filter option. Recognizing the importance of helping users find the correct hotel and room, I made the necessary addition to the prototype. This filter functionality would enable users to refine their search based on specific criteria such as location, price range, amenities, and more. By integrating this filter option, I aimed to streamline the user's journey and make it easier for them to find their ideal accommodation. The filter would act as a powerful tool, empowering users to narrow down their choices and quickly locate the options that align with their preferences and needs. Testing the prototype with the added filter option provided valuable insights into its effectiveness and usability. It allowed me to observe how users interacted with this new feature, gauging their satisfaction and identifying any potential pain points or areas for improvement.

My Learings

During the creation of the flow chart, I initially included all the steps required to complete the booking process. However, as I progressed to develop the low-fidelity design, I realized that certain steps could be eliminated to simplify the process. By enhancing the descriptions of the hotels and rooms, users can make informed decisions without unnecessary steps. In my effort to provide more information to users, I initially added an information button. However, through user testing, I discovered that this approach could lead to user frustration. To address this, I opted for a more streamlined approach. I incorporated a simple explanation directly accessible to users, while reserving more detailed information for the 'about' page. This ensures that users have the necessary information without overwhelming them with excessive details. While working on the wireframe, I recognized the need to make adjustments to the flow. To improve user navigation and flexibility, I included additional options that allow users to easily move back and forth within the process. This enhances the overall usability of the website and provides a more intuitive experience. Reflecting on my survey efforts, I realized that there were opportunities for improvement. The survey group was small, and the questions were not as well-thought-out as they could have been. To gather more meaningful feedback in the future, I will aim to expand the sample size and refine the survey questions, ensuring they are clear, comprehensive, and aligned with my research goals. This will result in more valuable insights that can inform the design process effectively. By recognizing and addressing these areas for improvement, I am committed to refining my design approach and delivering an exceptional user experience.
